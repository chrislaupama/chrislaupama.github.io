What is your experience of compassionate (or not) leadership?

In my teen years I had a boss who was very compassionate towards myseld and others. He even helped me in a personal situation (purchased me a car!) based on all the multitude of negatitve situations going on in my life at the time. He was one hell of a good man, and I always ensure I catch up with him at one of his restaurants when I'm in Wellington. I can certainly have experienced a compassionate leader first hand and can see how it makes an impact on people.

Do the multiplying goodness meditation. How did you find it?

It did seem nice, certainly will take me a while to do it in a purposeful state, however I can certainly understand the benefits.

Do the Tonglen meditation. How did you find it?

As it says in the book, this is certainly a bit harder to do as it seemd weird... inhaling the bad from myself, like I'm some demon? (lol) - HOWEVER, when I thought of someone else, it actually felt quite good, like I was actually helping them.

I'm glad that everyone is reading this book, makes me even more excited to meet everyone too actually.
