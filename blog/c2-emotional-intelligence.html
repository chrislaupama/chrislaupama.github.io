Emotional Intelligence

What learnings did you take from the chapter?

I found it very intereseting how emotional intelligence is something you can train and build upon, as well as the ability to recognise emotions and within certain situations to embrace them and allow you to have better control of them, especially in times of anger or stress etc.

How did you find the two minutes of mindfulness?

I must admit, it was new to me. Sitting there and being present and being aware of my breathing etc was very soothing and calming, I did find it interesting to actually really take notice that... I'm HERE, breathing, being... it was interesting to think like that.
