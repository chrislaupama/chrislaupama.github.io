Motivation

How does life look through the 'pleasure, passion and purpose' lens?

I found this concept very interesting and is certainly something I'm currently working through purposely. I believe that most people do focus a little through the pleasure lens a little more than the others, which isn't very sustainable. Passion is something I feel like I tend to sit in with purpose. However, I think that focusing on my purpose some more is something I want to focus on more. Basically focusing/working on something you like with an overall purpose and end goal is the way to be. Have a strong purpose on something I like, which will make me naturally work on it with passion and there will also be pleasurable moments throughout that whole process.

What are your core values?

Reliability, Honesty and Accountability are probably the core values I focus on and tend to be the ones that I naturally gravitate towards within others too.

What do you stand for?

Growth, within myself and others that surround me. No matter what it is, I like to see others around me grow, and I like to help them grow too. It's something that I have only recently started to fully put into myself but I like to help people and push them to take risks and achieve what they want to.

How did you find the resilience ideas and meditation exercise?

It seems pretty similar to the last chapters meditation session, however I can see how this will benefit me in the long run. Meditation is certainly something I'm going to continuely get used to.
